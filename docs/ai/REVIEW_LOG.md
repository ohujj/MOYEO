entries only when they improve the review harness.

- repeated false positive
- repeated missed risk

- policy/document mismatch occurred repeatedly
- repeated confusion between MVP shortcut and concrete post-MVP debt

document, or `docs/ai/CODE_REVIEW.md` is needed.
